# Hermertia → Hermertia Wars Import Policy

Hermertia is an open, community‑driven world licensed under  
**Creative Commons Attribution–ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY‑SA 4.0)**.

Hermertia Wars is a **separate, non‑canon commercial IP** inspired by Hermertia.  
This document explains how content may be imported from Hermertia into Hermertia Wars.

---

## 1. Separate Universes

Hermertia and Hermertia Wars exist as **parallel, non‑canon worlds**.

- Hermertia remains fully open, community‑governed, and share‑alike.  
- Hermertia Wars is a curated, proprietary cooperative IP.  
- Events, characters, and lore in one universe are not canon in the other unless explicitly imported.

This separation protects both the openness of Hermertia and the creative freedom of Hermertia Wars.

---

## 2. What Can Be Imported

The following types of Hermertia content may be adapted into Hermertia Wars:

- Characters  
- Realms and factions  
- Histories and timelines  
- Cultural lore  
- Maps and geography  
- Stories, events, and worldbuilding details  

Imported content may be modified, expanded, or reinterpreted to fit Hermertia Wars canon.

---

## 3. License of Imported Content

Hermertia content is licensed under **CC BY‑SA 4.0**.  
When imported into Hermertia Wars:

- The *lore layer* of the imported material remains CC BY‑SA.  
- The *game layer* (code, art, mechanics, UI, animations, cinematics, etc.) is fully proprietary.  
- Hermertia Wars may adapt, transform, or reinterpret Hermertia content while maintaining attribution.

This ensures Hermertia remains open while Hermertia Wars remains commercially viable.

---

## 4. Attribution

Hermertia Wars will provide attribution for imported Hermertia content in an appropriate and visible manner, such as:

- Credits section  
- Lore documentation  
- In‑game codex entries  
- Public acknowledgements  

Attribution will reference the Hermertia Commons and its contributors.

---

## 5. Optional Contributor Agreements

In some cases, Hermertia Wars may request additional rights beyond CC BY‑SA, such as:

- Exclusive commercial use  
- Use of a contributor’s likeness or voice  
- Integration of non‑lore creative assets (art, music, models, etc.)

These situations will use a simple, optional contributor agreement.  
Contributors are never required to sign anything to participate in Hermertia.

---

## 6. No Automatic Importing

Nothing from Hermertia becomes part of Hermertia Wars canon automatically.

Importing is always:

- **Selective**  
- **Intentional**  
- **Curated**  

Hermertia remains a free, open commons.  
Hermertia Wars remains a distinct, controlled IP.

---

## 7. Community Respect

Hermertia Wars will treat Hermertia content with respect for:

- the original creators  
- the shared history  
- the collaborative nature of the world  

Adaptations may diverge from Hermertia canon, but will honor the spirit of the original work.

---

## 8. Summary

- Hermertia is open (CC BY‑SA).  
- Hermertia Wars is proprietary.  
- Importing is allowed, curated, and attributed.  
- Imported lore stays CC BY‑SA; the game remains fully owned.  
- Contributor agreements are optional and only used when needed.  
- The universes are separate unless content is explicitly imported.

This policy protects the openness of Hermertia and the integrity of Hermertia Wars.
