# Hermertia Commons

Hermertia is a collaborative, community‑driven worldbuilding project.  
It is an open, shared lore universe built over many years by players, writers, builders, and storytellers.  
No single person or group owns Hermertia — it exists as a creative commons for everyone.

This GitHub organization serves as the home for Hermertia’s open‑license, and to support its lore, maps, histories, characters, and worldbuilding archives.

---

## 🌍 What Hermertia Is

- A shared world with no ownership  
- A living history shaped by community contributions  
- A place for realms, characters, stories, timelines, and cultural development  
- A commons that anyone can build on, remix, or expand  

Hermertia is licensed under **Creative Commons Attribution–ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY‑SA 4.0)**.  
This ensures the world remains open, remixable, and protected from exclusive ownership.

## 🔀 Relationship to Hermertia Wars

Hermertia Wars https://hermertiawars.com is a **separate, non‑canon commercial IP** inspired by Hermertia.  
Only content explicitly imported into Hermertia Wars becomes part of its cooperative IP.

Hermertia remains:
- Open  
- Community‑governed  
- Non‑owned  
- Share‑alike  

Hermertia Wars remains:
- Proprietary  
- Curated  
- Separate in canon
